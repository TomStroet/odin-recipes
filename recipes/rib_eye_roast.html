Rib Eye Roast

This delicious rib eye roast on the bone is a go-to for special dinners. It’s easy, flavorful, and always juicy—there are never any leftovers! We usually cook ours medium-rare.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you'll need to make this top-rated homemade lasagna recipe:

  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (4 pound) bone-in rib-eye roast
  • sea salt and cracked black pepper to taste

Steps

Making homemade lasagna much easier than it looks. You'll find the full, step-by-step recipe below — but here's a brief overview of what you can expect:

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 260 degrees C.
  2. Beat butter and garlic together in a small bowl until combined.
  3. Poke several holes in rib eye with a sharp knife. Rub butter mixture all over meat and season generously with salt and pepper. Place rib eye fat-side up in a roasting pan.
  4. Roast in the preheated oven for 20 minutes to develop a crust.
  5. Reduce heat to 165 degrees C and continue cooking until rib eye is reddish-pink and juicy in the center, 1 ½ to 2 hours. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center will read 63 degrees C for medium. Let rest before slicing.
  6. Serve and enjoy!
